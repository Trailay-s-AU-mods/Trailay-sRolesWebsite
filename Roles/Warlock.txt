{Warlock}
{Warlock is an Impostor, that can curse another player (the cursed player doesn't get notified). If the cursed person stands next to another player, the Warlock is able to kill that player (no matter how far away they are). Performing a kill with the help of a cursed player, will lift the curse and it will result in the Warlock being unable to move for a set amount of time}
